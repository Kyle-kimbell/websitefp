Device Inventory

Use this section to document every installed device, where it lives, and how it is managed.

Back to wiki

Core Fields

  • Device name and role
  • Manufacturer, model, and serial number
  • Physical location on site
  • IP address or network assignment
  • Install date and technician notes

Why It Matters

  • Speeds up troubleshooting
  • Makes replacements cleaner
  • Improves technician handoff
  • Gives customers a clear equipment record
  • Supports future upgrade planning
