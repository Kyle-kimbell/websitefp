Section 01

Device Inventory

Track installed equipment, where it is located, how it connects, and what needs support. This becomes the baseline record for every customer system.

Core Fields
  • Device name and role
  • Model and serial number
  • Location on site
  • IP address or network assignment
  • Install date and notes
Use Cases
  • Service troubleshooting
  • Replacement planning
  • Onboarding new technicians
  • Customer handoff documentation
  • Future upgrade planning
Section 02

Network Layout

Document how the customer site is connected so anyone servicing the account can understand coverage, uplinks, switches, radios, and critical routes quickly.

Include
  • ISP connection and handoff point
  • Main router and switch locations
  • Access points and bridge links
  • Static IP assignments and VLAN notes
  • Known weak spots or service limitations
Why It Matters
  • Faster troubleshooting
  • Cleaner change planning
  • Better technician handoff
  • Fewer site revisits
  • Stronger customer trust
Section 03

Customer SOP's

Capture the standard operating procedures the customer should follow for daily use, alerts, resets, and escalation paths.

Suggested SOPs
  • How to verify systems are online
  • What to do when an alert triggers
  • Who to contact for support
  • How to power cycle approved devices
  • When to escalate to North Valley Monitoring
Audience
  • Owners and managers
  • On-site operators
  • Maintenance staff
  • Service technicians
  • Future account contacts
Section 04

Maintenance

Keep routine maintenance visible so systems stay documented, updated, and supportable over time.

Recurring Tasks
  • Firmware and software review
  • Camera or sensor health check
  • Network uptime review
  • Credential review and updates
  • Physical inspection schedule
Record Keeping
  • Date completed
  • Technician name
  • What changed
  • Open issues found
  • Recommended follow-up
