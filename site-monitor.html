What It Monitors

A Practical Check on the Systems Farms Depend On

This service does not require custom farm hardware on day one. It monitors whether an online system is reachable and creates a record of outages, slow response times, and recoveries for the tools that support irrigation, field visibility, customer access, and daily operations.

01

Website Availability

Check whether a business website or farm portal returns a healthy response before customers or employees report that it is down.

02

Dashboard Reachability

Monitor cloud dashboards, camera pages, irrigation portals, or vendor systems that the operation depends on during the workday, especially when no one is sitting in front of them.

03

Uptime Reports

Export a simple status report showing checks, failures, response time, and recovery events. Useful for owners who want a record they can act on instead of another mystery outage.
