Services

Sensor Networks That Help Farmers Make Better Decisions

We install, configure, and maintain environmental monitoring equipment for small-to-medium farms who need reliable data but can't justify $50,000 enterprise ag-tech systems. Local service, straightforward pricing, sensors that actually work.

01

Soil Moisture Monitoring

Install soil probes at multiple depths to track exactly when crops need water. Eliminate guesswork on irrigation timing—know soil conditions without digging or walking fields.

02

Weather Station Installation

Micro-climate weather monitoring for frost alerts, heat stress warnings, wind speed, and temperature tracking. Get field-specific data—not just what's happening at the nearest airport.

03

Irrigation Monitoring

Water level sensors, flow meters, and pressure monitors for ponds, tanks, pumps, and irrigation lines. Catch problems before they cost you water, power, or crops.

04

Equipment & Asset Tracking

GPS tracking and usage monitors for tractors, pumps, vehicles, and high-value equipment. Know where your equipment is and when it's running.

05

Data Dashboard & Alerts

Monthly subscription includes cloud data hosting, mobile-friendly dashboards, SMS/email alerts, and quarterly maintenance visits to keep sensors calibrated.

06

Sensor Repair & Support

When sensors break, cables fail, or data stops coming in—we drive to your farm and fix it. No shipping equipment back, no phone trees, no waiting weeks for a technician.

Process

From Sensor Installation to Ongoing Local Support

Our process is designed for farmers who need working equipment, not technology for its own sake. We visit your farm, understand your irrigation and monitoring needs, install the right sensors, and stick around to make sure they keep working.

Step 1

On-Site Assessment

Walk your fields, understand your irrigation setup, identify the crops you grow, and figure out where sensors will give you the most useful data. No charge for initial consultation within 30 miles of Chico.

Step 2

Sensor Installation

Install soil moisture probes, weather stations, or monitoring equipment. Configure data transmission to the cloud. Test everything. Show you how to read the dashboard. Done in one day for most setups.

Step 3

Ongoing Monitoring & Support

Monthly subscription includes data hosting, dashboard access, automated alerts, and quarterly maintenance visits. When something breaks, call us—we show up.

About

Built for Farms That Need Data Without the Enterprise Overhead

North Valley Monitoring is a local agricultural monitoring service focused on the real-world needs of farms in California's North Valley. We serve almond growers, walnut farmers, rice operations, and specialty crop producers who want environmental data but don't have the budget or patience for complex ag-tech systems.

We're based in Chico and work within a 50-mile radius. We drive to your farm when sensors need attention. We answer the phone. We speak plain English about what the equipment does and what the data means.
