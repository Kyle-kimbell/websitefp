Custom smart farm systems.

North Valley Monitoring builds practical monitoring systems for farms that need better visibility into water, equipment, energy use, and remote sites. We design around the operation you already run, then install the sensors, alerts, and dashboards that make it easier to see what is happening.

What We Build

Farm systems that report before small problems get expensive.

We connect field hardware, internet-connected devices, and simple dashboards so growers can track the systems that keep the farm moving.

01

Irrigation visibility

Monitor pumps, tanks, flow, pressure, water levels, and field conditions so irrigation issues are easier to catch.

02

Equipment runtime

Track when fridges, pumps, fans, controllers, and other equipment are running, idle, offline, or acting outside normal patterns.

03

Alerts and dashboards

Send plain alerts and status views for the people who need them, whether they are in the shop, field, office, or truck.

How It Works

Built around your farm, not a generic software package.

Every farm has different equipment, weak spots, and routines. We start with the problem, choose the right sensors, and build a system that gives useful operating data without adding more busywork.

Step 1

Walk the operation

Find the systems that need better visibility and define what success looks like.

Step 2

Install the monitoring

Add the sensors, smart plugs, gateways, dashboards, and alerts that fit the job.

Step 3

Keep it useful

Review the data, adjust thresholds, and maintain the system as farm needs change.
