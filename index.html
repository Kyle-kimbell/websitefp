Services

Signals That Protect the Workday

We install, configure, and maintain monitoring equipment for small-to-medium farms that need useful data without a $50,000 enterprise ag-tech system. Local service, straightforward pricing, and alerts tied to the water, power, and equipment issues that cost growers real money.

01

Soil Moisture Monitoring

Install soil probes at multiple depths to track exactly when crops need water. Replace guesswork with field-level readings that help avoid dry zones, overwatering, and unnecessary pump time.

02

Weather Station Installation

Micro-climate weather monitoring for frost alerts, heat stress warnings, wind speed, and temperature tracking. Get field-specific data—not just what's happening at the nearest airport.

03

Irrigation Monitoring

Water level sensors, flow meters, and pressure monitors for ponds, tanks, pumps, and irrigation lines. Catch strange pressure, low levels, or missing flow before the next pass through the field.

04

Equipment & Asset Tracking

GPS tracking and usage monitors for tractors, pumps, vehicles, and high-value equipment. Know where critical assets are, when they are running, and when usage patterns need a closer look.

05

Data Dashboard & Alerts

Monthly subscription includes cloud data hosting, mobile-friendly dashboards, SMS/email alerts, and quarterly maintenance visits, so the readings stay useful after installation day.

06

Site Uptime Monitoring

Track whether farm websites, dashboards, routers, or remote systems are reachable. Get a plain uptime report and a local technician who can investigate when monitoring shows something dropped offline.

Process

From Field Check to Ongoing Local Support

Our process is designed for farmers who need working equipment, not technology for its own sake. We visit your farm, understand where water, pumps, power, and remote systems matter most, install the right monitoring, and stick around to keep it useful.

Step 1

On-Site Assessment

Walk your fields, understand your irrigation setup, identify the crops you grow, and figure out where sensors will give you the most useful data. No charge for initial consultation within 30 miles of Chico.

Step 2

Sensor Installation

Install soil moisture probes, weather stations, or monitoring equipment. Configure data transmission to the cloud. Test everything. Show you how to read the dashboard. Done in one day for most setups.

Step 3

Ongoing Monitoring & Support

Monthly subscription includes data hosting, dashboard access, automated alerts, and quarterly maintenance visits. When something breaks, call us—we show up.

About

North Valley Monitoring is a local agricultural monitoring service focused on the real-world needs of farms in California's North Valley.
